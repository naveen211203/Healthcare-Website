NATUROPATHY

Naturopathy is to change the unhealthy living habits of people and to maintain healthy and positive life style.It is a form of alternative medicine that emphasizes on the use of natural remedies to help the body heal itself. It is based on the belief that the body has an innate ability to heal and that illness is caused by imbalances or blockages in the body's natural processes. Naturopathic practitioners use a variety of therapies including herbal medicine, homeopathy, nutrition, hydrotherapy, and acupuncture to promote wellness and prevent disease.

Naturopathy is a type of medicine that is based on the nature resources. Naturopathy has been used to treat a wide range of health conditions, including allergies, chronic pain, digestive disorders, hormonal imbalances, and stress-related conditions.Naturopathy treatment may includes nutritional medicine , diet advice , herbal medicine , homeopathy.
